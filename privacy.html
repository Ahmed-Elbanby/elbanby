elbanby.com ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using our website, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us. This includes:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, phone number (optional), service interest, and message content.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and the page you visited — collected automatically for security and abuse prevention purposes only.

We do not collect payment information, create user accounts, or track your behaviour across other websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your project enquiry.
  • Understand the type of service you need.
  • Prevent spam and abuse of our contact form.

We do not use your information for marketing emails, sell your data to third parties, or share it with anyone outside elbanby.com except as required by law.

3. Data Storage & Security

Contact form submissions are stored in a private database accessible only to the elbanby.com team. We apply technical measures including:

  • Encrypted database connections (TLS/SSL)
  • Parameterised SQL queries to prevent injection attacks
  • Rate limiting to prevent automated abuse
  • Server access restricted by IP allowlist

We retain contact form data for up to 12 months, after which it is deleted unless it relates to an active client engagement.

4. Third Parties

Our website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): anonymous usage statistics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Font Awesome (CDN): icon fonts served from Cloudflare CDN.
  • Google Fonts: typography loaded from Google Fonts servers.

We do not embed social media trackers, advertising pixels, or any data broker integrations on this website.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@elbanby.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies:

  • Language preference: stored in localStorage under the key elbanby_lang to remember your language choice. This never leaves your device and is not a tracking cookie.
  • Session cookie (PHP): a temporary session cookie used only for CSRF protection on the contact form. It expires when you close your browser.

We do not use advertising cookies, fingerprinting, or cross-site tracking.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at:

elbanby.com
Email: info@elbanby.com

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
