Our story

We Started with a Simple Idea

elbanby.com was founded in 2021 with one belief: great software changes everything. What started as a small web development operation quickly grew as clients realized they could trust us to build things right.

Today we're a focused team of developers and designers delivering web apps, mobile applications, APIs, and digital experiences for clients across the region — with quality and honesty at the center of everything we do.
